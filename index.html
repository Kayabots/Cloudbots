About Scuti

Revolutionizing how the world learns, earns, and connects.

At Scuti, we believe that technology, truth and transformation can co-exist to uplift humanity. Our journey began in Zurich between 2012-2015, where founder Frank Ferguson first explored intelligent systems that helped users manage tasks, book tickets and handle payments—an early spark that would lay the foundation of what Scuti would eventually become. From 2019-2023 in Madrid, Frank shifted focus to personal development and spiritual growth, writing The Power of Transfocreation and transforming the idea of an assistant into a mentor: one that supports you mentally, physically, spiritually and financially.

Between 2022-2024, Scuti’s vision met innovation through a collaboration in Oman with Dr Abdullah Al Zakwani and his technical team, creating our MVP with AI-recommendation systems, chat, booking and geolocation features. This phase forged the global-ready architecture of the platform. Today — in 2025 and beyond — Scuti has launched globally from our Barcelona base, merging EdTech, SportsTech and FinTech under a blockchain-driven framework, with VR on the horizon. With the backing of Nexen Capital and a dedicated global team, we are connecting students, coaches and businesses across continents.

Scuti — Beyond the Stars. Created to guide humanity through technology, truth and transformation.
