Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans at any time?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any charges.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise accounts. Your customers can pay you via tokens or cash through the app.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No! All plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime with no penalties. Enterprise plans may have custom terms.

How do venue partnerships work?

You can partner with local businesses (cafes, gyms, etc.) to host sessions or send customers. You earn commissions based on traffic generated.

What's included in the AWS Marketplace listing?

Full SaaS platform license, white-label options, deployment on your AWS infrastructure, and dedicated support. Perfect for corporations wanting their own branded platform.

Do you offer discounts for annual payments?

Yes! Pay annually and get 2 months free on Professional and Business plans. Contact sales for enterprise annual contracts.
